Website Evaluation

Worst Website

This website does not follow the principles of CRAP

  • C - Contrast

  • The website has too many colors, making it hard to focus on one thing at a time. It's tough to read.

  • R - Repetition

  • The website's left side has a bunch of tiny links stacked on top of each other. It gets repetitive and boring for users to look at.

  • A - Alignment

  • The pictures are arranged like a collage, but they're not lined up right, so they look weird and you can only really focus on one thing at a time.

  • P - Proximity

  • The page is too crowded, there's no free space except on the sides.

Best Website

This website does follow the principles of CRAP

  • C - Contrast

  • The website has a balanced and coordinated color palette, making it easy to differentiate between different sections and elements. It's visually pleasing and enhances the overall user experience.

  • R - Repetition

  • The website's left side has a clear and organized layout, with links that are consistently spaced and easy to find. This creates a sense of familiarity for users and ensures a smooth navigation experience.

  • A - Alignment

  • The pictures are arranged in a way that is pleasing to the eye, with each element aligned and balanced. This creates a sense of harmony and allows users to appreciate each element individually and as part of the whole design.

  • P - Proximity

  • The page has a good balance of content and empty space, with ample breathing room around each element. This creates a sense of clarity and allows users to focus on each individual piece of content without feeling overwhelmed

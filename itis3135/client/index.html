What is Soccer?

Soccer, also known as football, is a popular sport played worldwide. It involves two teams of eleven players each, trying to score goals by kicking a ball into the opposing team's net.

Basic Rules of Soccer

  • Each team aims to score more goals than the other team.
  • Players cannot touch the ball with their hands or arms (except the goalkeeper within their penalty area).
  • A match consists of two halves, typically 45 minutes each.
  • A player receives a yellow card for a caution and a red card for a dismissal.
  • The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.
